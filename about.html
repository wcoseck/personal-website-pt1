Writing and Reading

Writing and reading have always been one of the personal interests that I hold the dearest. For some time I tried becoming a writer, a children's book author, and a journalist. I started by writing a collection of poems that hold a powerful message that try to build a reader's analytical skills through the use rhetorical devices. The idea behind the collection was to build poems and books that are unusual for kids to read, they were suppose to be a breathe of fresh air. Explicit forms of writing were suppose to illustrate real life experiences to children instead of using poor choice of vocabulary.
